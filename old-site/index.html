I am a passionate programmer who is always looking for ways to gain experience and grow my knowledge base.

Projects

In the summer of 2015, I worked at DC-based company SocialRadar. I developed an example app to demonstrate LocationKit (their location-tracking iOS SDK), which can be found here.
I am actively developing TJIntranet, an iOS application made for my school's intranet. It provides an easy way to view the day's schedule, sign up for eighth periods, and view announcements.
I developed the game Paddle-Pong, a game where the player uses a paddle to hit a ball into one of four border walls.
